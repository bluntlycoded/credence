Service 06

FBAR & FATCA Filing

FBAR: The Foreign Bank Account Report requires U.S. persons with foreign financial accounts exceeding $10,000 to report them annually using FinCEN Form 114. This applies to bank accounts, brokerage accounts, and certain foreign retirement plans.

FATCA: The Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act requires reporting of specified foreign financial assets on Form 8938 if thresholds are met. Compliance with both requirements is critical to avoid severe penalties.

FBAR filing (FinCEN Form 114)
FATCA reporting (Form 8938)
Delinquent filing procedures
Streamlined filing compliance
Voluntary disclosure assistance
